This is me.

Hello there!


I am glad you are visiting my site and took the time to read this section. This is where I tell a little bit about myself!

I am born and raised in Brussels and currently finishing my bachelor Illustration at LUCA School Of Arts in Ghent. 

Drawing has always been my passion. Subconsiously I always tried to bring a story or a feeling across through my drawings. I first studied six years of Visual Arts in Sint Lukas Brussels. It wasn't untill my fifth year that I found out about Illustration in Ghent. That's when I realised that I wanted to tell stories.

I love melancholy, and it is something I try to incorporate in my work. Most of my work is done in either chinese ink or ecoline. But I also love printmaking and I hope to use it more in the future!
